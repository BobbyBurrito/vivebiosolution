WINDSOR-BASED CLEANING PRODUCT MANUFACTURER

Professional Cleaning Products Built for Real Working Environments

Vive Biosolution Corporation is a Windsor-based manufacturer and supplier of professional bio-derived cleaning products for industrial, commercial, janitorial, food-service, and facility maintenance applications.

We started Vive Biosolution with a simple goal: to give local businesses access to high-performance cleaning products that are effective, practical, and manufactured with a stronger focus on safer, lower-impact ingredients. Our products are designed for real working environments — machine shops, restaurants, offices, warehouses, maintenance teams, cleaning contractors, and commercial facilities.

From industrial degreasers to ammonia-free glass cleaners, floor cleaners, all-purpose cleaners, dish soap, grill cleaners, and bulk cleaning solutions, our focus is on dependable performance, local supply, and honest product support.

WHY WE EXIST

Local Supply. Practical Formulas. Professional Performance.

As a Canadian manufacturer based in Windsor, Ontario, we understand the needs of businesses across Windsor-Essex, Chatham-Kent, and Ontario. We offer products in 4L jugs, 20L pails, and bulk supply options for companies that need reliable cleaning products without unnecessary supply chain delays.

Our mission is to support businesses with professional cleaning solutions that combine performance, practicality, and responsible formulation — without overcomplicated claims or inflated pricing.
