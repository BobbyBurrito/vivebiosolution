The Science

Not Chemistry That Moves Grease.
Biology That Destroys It.

Chemical drain cleaners dissolve FOG temporarily — it re-solidifies downstream in your pipes. Our bacteria convert FOG to CO₂ and water. It disappears permanently.

01
0 — 2 Hours After Dosing
Enzymatic Emulsification

Bromelain and lipase enzymes activate within 2 hours of dosing, breaking triglyceride ester bonds and emulsifying the FOG layer into microscopic droplets 1,000× more accessible to bacterial attack. Plant-based decyl glucoside surfactant supports immediate emulsification.

02
2 — 24 Hours
Bacterial Germination & Colony Formation

Bacillus spores germinate, detecting warmth, moisture, and peptone nutrients. Quorum sensing activation triggers full biofilm mode immediately — establishing a colony on trap walls 60% faster than standard bio-enzymatic products.

03
Day 3 — Day 7
Beta-Oxidation — Grease to CO₂ + H₂O

Bacteria process fatty acids through beta-oxidation. Complete mineralization: C₅₁H₉₈O₆ + 72.5 O₂ → 51 CO₂ + 49 H₂O. The grease is completely destroyed — not relocated to the municipal sewer.

04
Day 14 Onwards
Self-Sustaining Biological System

Your grease trap becomes a living biological reactor — continuously digesting incoming FOG with no additional intervention beyond weekly maintenance doses. Colony self-maintains, extending pump-out intervals 2–3×.
