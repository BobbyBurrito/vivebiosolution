Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I see results?

Most customers notice improved drainage and reduced odors within the first week of use. Full benefits are typically realized within 2-3 weeks.

Is it safe for my equipment?

Yes! Our products are 100% safe for all plumbing and equipment. They're chemical-free and won't damage pipes or septic systems.

What's the shelf life?

Our products have a 24-month shelf life when stored in cool, dry conditions. Check the label for the specific expiration date.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer competitive bulk pricing for large orders. Contact us for a custom quote based on your volume needs.

How do I know which product to choose?

VIVE-DRAIN is best for restaurants and food service. VIVE-SHIFT is ideal for industrial and manufacturing applications. Contact us if you're unsure.

Absolutely! Our team provides free technical support, dosing protocols, and custom solutions for your specific needs.
