The Best Industrial Degreaser for CNC Shops in Windsor: Why Butyl-Free is the Future

Published by VIVE BIOSOLUTION | May 23, 2026

In the bustling industrial landscape of Windsor, Ontario, CNC machining and metal fabrication shops are the backbone of many industries. Traditionally, many shops have relied on degreasers containing 2-Butoxyethanol, commonly known as butyl. However, modern chemistry offers superior, safer alternatives. VIVE BIOSOLUTION is at the forefront, providing bio-based, butyl-free industrial degreasers specifically engineered for the unique challenges of CNC shops.

The Problem with Traditional Butyl-Based Degreasers

For decades, butyl-based degreasers were the go-to for cutting through tough machining oils, coolants, and heavy grease. While effective, they come with significant drawbacks:

  • Health Concerns: 2-Butoxyethanol is classified as a reproductive hazard, posing risks to workers through inhalation and skin exposure. In enclosed shop environments, these risks are amplified.
  • Harsh Odors: The strong, often unpleasant fumes associated with traditional degreasers can lead to poor air quality, worker discomfort, and potential respiratory issues.
  • Environmental Impact: Many traditional formulations are not readily biodegradable and can contribute to environmental pollution, making drain compliance more challenging.

Why VIVE-SHIFT is the Superior Choice for CNC Shops

VIVE-SHIFT, VIVE BIOSOLUTION's flagship industrial degreaser, is engineered to address these concerns without compromising on performance. It represents a new era of cleaning chemistry, offering a powerful, bio-based solution that is safer for both workers and the environment.

Key Advantages of VIVE-SHIFT:

  • Butyl-Free Formulation: VIVE-SHIFT completely eliminates 2-Butoxyethanol, removing the reproductive hazard classification and creating a healthier workspace for your team. This is a critical factor for any responsible CNC shop in Windsor looking to prioritize worker well-being.
  • Low VOC (Volatile Organic Compound) Content: With a VOC content of just 3.8%, VIVE-SHIFT significantly reduces airborne emissions. This means improved air quality in enclosed machining areas, leading to greater worker comfort and reduced health risks.
  • Bio-Surfactant Technology: Our formula utilizes advanced bio-surfactants that are biodegradable and highly effective at penetrating and lifting stubborn oils, grease, and coolants. This not only ensures a thorough clean but also aids in easier drain compliance.
  • Citrus D-Limonene Bio-Solvent: Harnessing the natural degreasing power of citrus, VIVE-SHIFT cuts through heavy contamination on metal surfaces, CNC equipment, and shop floors with remarkable efficiency. This natural solvent provides powerful cleaning without the harshness of synthetic alternatives.
  • Fast-Acting & Reduced Dwell Time: VIVE-SHIFT is designed for rapid action, minimizing the time required for the degreaser to penetrate grime. This reduces manual scrubbing and overall cleaning downtime, boosting productivity in your CNC shop.
  • WHMIS 2015 / GHS Compliant: Safety is paramount. VIVE-SHIFT comes with a fully compliant Safety Data Sheet (SDS), providing transparent and essential information for workplace safety protocols.
  • Made in Canada: Proudly engineered and manufactured in Windsor, Ontario, VIVE-SHIFT supports local industry while adhering to stringent Canadian quality standards.

Applications in Your CNC Shop

VIVE-SHIFT is versatile and effective across various applications in a CNC machining or metal fabrication environment:

  • CNC Equipment: Safely cleans lathes, mills, and other precision machinery, removing cutting fluids and metalworking lubricants.
  • Metal Surfaces: Ideal for degreasing raw materials, fabricated parts, and finished components before further processing or finishing.
  • Shop Floors: Effectively removes oil spills, grease buildup, and general industrial grime from concrete and epoxy floors, improving safety and cleanliness.
  • Tool & Parts Cleaning: Excellent for cleaning tools, jigs, and smaller parts, ensuring they are free from contaminants.

Choosing the right industrial degreaser is a critical decision for any CNC shop in Windsor.
